Exit from Windows

Run parameters:
/lock - lock workstation (such as Win+L);
/sleep - sleep system;
/sleep-awake - such as sleep and wake events enabled;
/hibernate - hibernate system;
/hibernate-awake - such as hibernate and wake events enabled;
/logoff - shuts down all processes running in the logon session;
/logoff-force - such as logoff and forced closing apps without warning;
/shutdown - shutdown the system;
/shutdown-force - such as shutdown and forced closing apps without warning;
/hybrid - hybrid shutdown for fast startup (supported: Win8+, previous OS ignored);
/hybrid-force - such as hybrid and forced closing apps without warning;
/reboot - restart the system;
/reboot-force - such as restart and forced closing apps without warning.
