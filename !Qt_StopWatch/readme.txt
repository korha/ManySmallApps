Stopwatch and countdown timer

Run parameters:
[%number%] - Use format number for display time. %number% must be between 1 and 16 (used default 15)

Available formats:
1) hh:mm:ss
2) hh:mm:ss.x
3) hh:mm:ss.xx
4) hh:mm:ss.xxx
5) h:mm:ss
6) h:mm:ss.x
7) h:mm:ss.xx
8) h:mm:ss.xxx
9) mm:ss
10) mm:ss.x
11) mm:ss.xx
12) mm:ss.xxx
13) m:ss
14) m:ss.x
15) m:ss.xx
16) m:ss.xxx

Where the expressions mean:
h - The hour without a leading zero (0 to 23)
hh - The hour with a leading zero (00 to 23)
m - The minute without a leading zero (0 to 59)
mm - The minute with a leading zero (00 to 59)
ss - The second with a leading zero (00 to 59)
x - Tenth of a second
xx - Hundredth of a second
xxx - Thousandth of a second
